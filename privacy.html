Privacy Policy

ClubFitter Pro · Last updated June 2026

ClubFitter Pro is designed to work entirely offline. We take your privacy seriously.

Data Collection

ClubFitter Pro does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage data, or analytics. The app does not use third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking technologies.

Information You Enter

Any swing data, equipment selections, or other information you enter into ClubFitter Pro (such as swing speed, launch angle, or equipment preferences) is stored only on your device and is never transmitted anywhere. Uninstalling the app permanently removes this data.

Third-Party Services

ClubFitter Pro does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-collection services.

Children's Privacy

ClubFitter Pro does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@clubfitterpro.com.
