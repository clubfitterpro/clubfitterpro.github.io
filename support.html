Frequently Asked Questions

Does ClubFitter Pro require an internet connection?
No — ClubFitter Pro works fully offline. All calculations run on your device.

Where do I enter my swing data?
Go to the Driver or Irons tab and enter your launch monitor numbers (swing speed, ball speed, launch angle, spin rate). The app will calculate smash factor, swing weight, and carry distance automatically.

How accurate are the recommendations?
ClubFitter Pro uses physics-based formulas calibrated against published launch monitor data. It's designed to give you a strong starting point for a professional fitting — not a replacement for one.

Privacy

ClubFitter Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information you enter stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.
