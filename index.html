What it does

Three places golfers leave yards on the table.

Enter the numbers you already have — or the ones your last fitting gave you — and get a plain-English read on what's working and what to change.

Driver

Smash factor, swing weight & launch

Enter swing speed and ball speed to see your smash factor instantly, plus whether your launch and spin sit inside the window for your speed — and what head or shaft change would help most.

Irons

Carry distance & yardage gapping

See your 7-iron carry, swing weight, and whether your set is gapped in even steps — or if a strong-lofted set has quietly created a gap between your wedges and irons.

Ball fit

The cheapest upgrade in golf

Match ball compression to your swing speed. For most players this is a $7–17 per sleeve change that's worth more than most equipment upgrades — and it's the first thing the report checks.
